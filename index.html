Domestic cat


Kitty origins

People began to domesticate cats in the Fertile Crescent about 10.000 years ago, according to DNA research.
Modern-day cats descend from a subspecies of African Wildcat, Felis silvestris lybica, which today is the most common and widespread wildcat.

Fun cat facts

Our furry little feline friends are sometimes a mystery, however here are some facts we do know about them!

  1. A house cat is genetically 95.6% tiger.
  2. Cats can jump 5 times their height.
  3. Each cat's nose is unique, much like human fingerprints.
  4. Cats can dream.
  5. Cats sleep 12-16 hours per day, and spend 1/3 of their awake hours grooming.
